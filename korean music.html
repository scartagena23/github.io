Korean Music

You are probably wondering why korean music is listed as one of my insterests and how did this happen. One day I went down the YouTube rabbit hole and accidently listened to it and fell in love. I have to say I'm mainly a fan of kpop. Kpop is Korean popular music. It is mainstream music that originated from South Korea. Korean pop music in Korea could be thought of as similar to Taylor Swift in the United States. You have probably heard the song "Gangnam Style", if you have well that is kpop. My favorite band is BTS. Do I understand what they are singing? Of course not, I have to google lyric translation but their music just brings happiness to my life.
